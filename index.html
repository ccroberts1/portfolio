Hello! My name is Caitlin and I'm a freelance web developer based out of Denver, CO. My path to development was partially self-taught, as someone who was always interested in the challenge of building new tools and websites. I also recently enrolled in University of Denver's Coding Bootcamp to finally make the jump over to development.
